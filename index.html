Welcome to the unofficial Youtube Game

Playing this game with friends is a entertaining way,
to discover videos and especially music on youtube.
Sit together with friends and show them your favourite videos.
Let them rate your taste of music, and rate your friends taste.

Find the song of the evening, and appoint the person
with the best feeling for what the others like.
  1. Start adding players
  2. The first player searches for a video and pushes the "Take This Video"-button
  3. Now the other players have to rate this video from min. 1 to max. 10 points
  4. After all ratings are done, push the "End This Round"-button
  5. The player who selected the video gets the sum of the points.
  6. The round has ended.
  7. Now the next player can start searching for a video
  8. Play as many rounds as you want....
  9. In the end, the player who has most points is the winner.
